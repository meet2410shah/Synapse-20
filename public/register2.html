Group Events
Participants might choose to register as a group for any of the following genre of events. Note that if a group has registered for a particular event, the group registration valid only for that particular event and category. If a member of the group wishes to participate in a different category of same event (eg. solo/duo), seperate charges would be applied.
1. Rampage (Rs 1000 per group)

Team fashion event. Budding designers express their creative independence and boldness as they illustrate their stories while the most glamorous models walk to the pumping beats.

2. Masquerade (Drama) (Rs 200)

All members of the group will be able to participate in any one​ of the following events.

  • Naivete (Street Play)
  • Proscenium (Stage Play)
  • Felicity (Mime)

Note that members of this group can participate in other listed events with additional registration fees.
Apart from this, all members of the group will be able to participate in any or all of the following events:

  • Comique(Stand-up)
